- **Slack**: Join us on [Kubernetes Slack](https://kubernetes.slack.com/) in the `#prometheus-operator` channel
- **Development**: For development-related discussions, use the `#prometheus-operator-dev` channel
- **Office Hours**: We hold [bi-weekly contributor office hours](https://github.com/prometheus-operator/prometheus-operator/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md#office-hours-meetings)

## Joining the Team

We welcome new contributors to join the Prometheus-Operator team! Here's how you can get started:

1. **Start Contributing**: Pick up [issues](https://github.com/prometheus-operator/prometheus-operator/issues) and comment on them to express your interest. Discuss your approach with maintainers before starting work
2. **Engage with the Community**: Help others on Slack, review pull requests, and participate in discussions
3. **Attend Office Hours**: Join our [bi-weekly meetings](https://github.com/prometheus-operator/prometheus-operator/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md#office-hours-meetings) to connect with maintainers and other contributors
4. **Consistent Contributions**: Demonstrate sustained commitment through quality contributions over time

For detailed information on the contributor ladder and team membership, see the [governance](https://github.com/prometheus-operator/prometheus-operator/blob/main/governance.md) document.
